The Concept

A focused croque sandwich offer.

CROQO takes a familiar French café product and adapts it into a focused grab-and-go format. The menu is deliberately tight, allowing for speed, consistency and strong quality control in compact, high-footfall sites.


The product has broad market appeal across residents, tourists, students, office workers and commuters. It is recognisable, easy to understand and more distinctive than a standard sandwich or generic fast-food offer.

Operating Model

Light-touch and controlled.

CROQO is planned as an electric-only operation with no open flame and no heavy extraction requirement. The setup is intended to be tidy, compact and suitable for kiosk-led trading.
